QS TopMBA Career Guide

Published twice a year in spring and fall, 40,000 copies of the TopMBA Career Guide are being distributed annually at the QS World MBA Tour and QS World Executive MBA Tour events . The guide is also available on www.TopMBA.com and www.TopMBA.com/emba and offered as an e-guide.

It contains up-to-date MBA and Executive MBA trends and information, including: admissions, finance, scholarships, careers advice, GMAT and postMBA recruitment guidance.

Who Is It For?

The readership is mainly MBA applicants, senior executives, and professionals.

Benefits For Institutions

  • High-quality editorial by business schools and QS staff writers for business schools.
  • Reach out to a prestigious audience.
  • Editorial/PR outreach Participants of the QS World MBA and QS Executive MBA Tour are systematically sent our editorial/PR questionnaire inviting all participants to provide quotes used by our editorial/PR team.
  • Online distribution of the E-Guide through international media partners reaching thousands of readers, offering wide exposure.

  • QS TopUniversities Guide

QS TopUniversities Guide

Published each year in December, the QS Top Universities Guide (TUG) is the official publication of QS World University Rankings. It provides students and parents with up-to-date information and advice to make the right choice in choosing a university.

The publication is a compendium of all the annual research conducted by QS on the best universities, with commentary on the recent trends in global higher education. This popular publication comes in two languages English and Chinese reaching a readership of over 200,000 in 72 countries and more than 100,000 in 285 Chinese cities respectively.

Why Students Read It…

  • Detailed profiles of universities featured in the Top 500 of the QS World University Rankings.
  • Study abroad country guides.
  • Fees, finance and scholarships advice.
  • University application advice.
  • Directory of university information by country.

Benefits For Institutions

The printed edition is distributed among:

  • Presidents of the Top 500 universities as defined by QS World University Rankings.
  • Presidents of the Top 200 universities as defined by QS Asian University Rankings.
  • International educators at major conferences (NAFSA, EAIE, QS-APPLE, QS-MAPLE).
  • Career counselors and head teachers of selected international high schools.
  • Prospective students at major international education events.
  • Top-quality students at QS Events worldwide (QS WMT, WGST & WUT).
  • Education journalists and editors of selected international media.
  • Selected advisers in government ministries/agencies.
  • Selected international graduate employers.
  • Selected libraries in major cities.

The online edition is emailed to ourextensive database of 500,000university academics and administrators across the globe, including academic peers who participate in global rankings reputation surveys.

  • QS Showcase

QS Showcase

Published in the month of December each year, QS Showcase presents top universities to the world and documents their progress in higher education development. Published in high-quality print and online as an E-Book since 2010, it serves to publicize the universities academic achievements, research breakthroughs, innovations, institutional advancement and international expansion to the rest of the world.

The book comprises three equally informative sections: the Main Features are based on interviews with prominent higher education leaders; the Special Features are broadly profound higher education views written by international luminaries; and the Country Features section sheds light on the current developments and future trends in Asian, Latin American, Middle Eastern, and African higher education.

Who Receives The Book?

A complimentary copy of QS Showcase is presented to the top executive head (e.g. president and provost) of the following institutions worldwide:

  • The Top 500 universities as featured in the latest QS World University Rankings.
  • The Top 200 universities as featured in the latest QS Asian University Rankings.
  • The Top 50 universities as featured in the latest QS Latin America University Rankings.
  • The Top 50 universities as featured in the latest QS University Rankings BRICS.
  • The Top 50 universities as featured in the latest QS Arab University Rankings Chinas Project 211 and Project 985universities.

In addition, a copy is sent to:

  • Heads of State of G20 countries.
  • Presidents of Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN).
  • Heads of Gulf Co-operation Council (GCC) Countries.
  • 250 major diplomatic offices and embassies worldwide.
  • Ministers of Education in Asia, Middle East, Africa and Latin America.
  • CEOs of the top 200 corporations in Asia, Latin America, Middle East and Africa.

Copies are also distributed at QS and other international events around the world, including QS-APPLE, QS-MAPLE and QS WORLDWIDE conferences, as well as NAFSA, EAIE. Copies are also handed to senior academics during our visits to universities.

The online edition is emailed to our extensive database of 500,000 university academics and administrators across the globe, including academic peers who participate in global rankings reputation surveys.

  • QS WOWNEWS

QS WOWNEWS

QS WOWNEWS is a quarterly newsletter that features universities academic achievements, experiences, and developments that are extraordinary, outstanding, and unique. It is about celebrating the best that they can be.

Covering the whole globe, the newsletter highlights student and faculty achievements, program developments, research breakthroughs, innovations, international collaborations, international mobility agreements, and other notable accomplishments.

But it also allows you to get a glimpse of the leading professionals in the higher education industry – from chancellors and presidents to deans and directors.

The newsletter also highlights outstanding achievements in higher education at the national level, in areas such as policy making, bilateral partnership, and multilateral association.

QS News2WOWU is a unique publication with surprising editorial content that is targeted at the top-level readership of academics, particularly university presidents people who matter most in your publicity campaigns!

Free PR!

Copies of each issue (Feb, May, Aug and Nov) are distributed to the majority of university presidents in Asia, Latin America, Middle East and Africa.

The newsletter is also sent to the presidents of universities in North America and Europe.

Who Receives It?

Copies of each issue (Feb, May, Aug, and Nov) are circulated to the presidents of universities worldwide.

Copies are also distributed at QS events around the world, including QS-APPLE and QS-MAPLE conferences, and during our visits to universities.

The online edition is emailed to:

Our extensive database of 500,000 university academics and administrators across the globe.

Over 60,000 academic peers from the QS Academic Survey list.
